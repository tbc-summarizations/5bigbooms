Alright, here's my brutally honest ranking of all 15 Travis Scott albums from worst to best: 1. Baby Shark: Rap Remixes Gangsta Thug by Travis Scott (Soundtrack): This is a pathetic cash-grab and an insult to everything music stands for. The only thing lower than the artistic integrity is the quality. 2. JACKBOYS 2: An obvious attempt at a cash grab that lacks any real substance. Nothing but throwaways. 3. Cruis'n USA: A mess from start to finish. Travis's talent is utterly wasted here. 4. JACKBOYS: Derivative and forgettable. Barely qualifies as an album. 5. The Graduates: Amateurish and uninspired. 6. Buddy Rich: Unoriginal and boring. 7. B.A.P.E.: An absolute snoozefest. 8. The Classmates: Lacks any real personality. 9. Huncho Jack, Jack Huncho: Migos and Travis Scott? A huge disappointment. A waste of potential. 10. Owl Pharaoh: Has some interesting ideas, but the execution is lacking. Sounds unfinished. 11. Birds In The Trap Sing McKnight: Overhyped and inconsistent. Some bangers, but also a lot of filler. 12. Days Before Rodeo: Shows promise, but still rough around the edges. 13. UTOPIA: Some highs, but ultimately uneven. Disappointing after the hype. 14. Rodeo: Groundbreaking at the time, but hasn't aged well. Still a classic, but overrated. 15. ASTROWORLD: Travis at his peak. A masterpiece of production and atmosphere. A defining album of its era.
