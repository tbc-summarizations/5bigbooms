Alright, buckle up, here's my unfiltered take on these flags: 1. Iceland: 9/10. Simple, clean, and effective. The Nordic cross design is iconic, and the colors are a beautiful representation of Iceland's landscape. It's a flag that oozes strength and history without being cluttered. Absolutely love it. 2. Ireland: 6/10. The tricolor design is fine, but it's honestly a bit boring. The colors themselves don't have enough visual impact. It's just... there. Could be more interesting. 3. Italy: 5/10. Almost identical to Ireland flag and it is just plain lazy design wise. This is almost unforgivable. 4. Kazakhstan: 10/10. Now this is a flag! The vibrant blue, the golden sun and eagle... it's bold, memorable, and full of symbolism. It screams "strength and pride" while also being visually stunning. A masterpiece of flag design. 5. Latvia: 4/10. Oh, the maroon and white? It's just bland. It lacks any visual appeal, and honestly, it's forgettable. Needs a serious redesign. 6. Liechtenstein: 3/10. Seriously? Just two blocks of color with a tiny crown? It's uninspired and screams "we couldn't be bothered." The crown is an afterthought. 7. Lithuania: 7/10. The colors are interesting and distinct, but the overall design is still a bit too simple. It lacks the boldness of some other flags. 8. Luxembourg: 5/10. This is basically just a copy of the Netherlands' flag but with different colors. Unoriginal. 9. Malta: 2/10. This is the worst flag I have seen so far. It is visually appalling and unappealing. 10. Moldova: 6/10. The tricolor is okay, but the coat of arms is way too detailed and busy for a flag. It clashes with the simplicity of the stripes and makes it look cluttered. Could be much better with a simplified emblem.
