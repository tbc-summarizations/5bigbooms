Alright, buckle up because I'm about to dissect these logos with the seriousness they (hopefully) deserve: 1. Norway: 9/10 - This logo is fantastic. It elegantly incorporates the Norwegian flag, the lions give it a regal touch, and the "NORGE" text is clean and authoritative. It perfectly represents the nation's identity. 2. Czech Republic: 7/10 - Simple, but effective. The rampant lion is a classic heraldic symbol, representing strength and courage. The color scheme is bold. Overall, a solid representation of Czech identity. 3. Greece: 3/10 - What is this?! This looks like a cheap logo that belongs on a random T-shirt from a flea market. The design is uninspired, and the strange geometric shapes have no clear connection to Greek history or culture. The text is the only acceptable element. 4. Scotland: 8/10 - I love this logo! The gold color with the burgundy lion is bold and the thistles give it a unique Scottish flair. Also the fact that the establishment year is on the logo is really cool. 5. Romania: 6/10 - It's visually interesting and a bit chaotic, much like the country's history, but it lacks a cohesive feel. The elements are crammed together, and the overall design feels somewhat amateurish. 6. Slovakia: 5/10 - This design is a mess. The giant soccer ball, the poorly rendered national symbol, and the overwhelming text make this logo an eyesore. It lacks sophistication and feels outdated. 7. Slovenia: 6/10 - The rings, mountains, and stars are decent, but nothing else is interesting or stands out. It's a safe design, but could be better. 8. Ireland: 8/10 - The Shamrock is a classic symbol and that symbol is represented in the best possible way. The white and green is iconic and also the added touch of orange on the rim shows that there is no favoritism to religion. 9. Albania: 7/10 - Another logo with the double headed eagle, but this one is much better than the North Macedonia one. The detail on the eagle is good and the color scheme is great. 10. North Macedonia: 3/10 - This logo is an absolute failure. It's a visual insult to the nation it represents. The typeface is horrible, the sphere in the middle is generic and the logo's color doesn't make any sense. Everything about it is dreadful.
