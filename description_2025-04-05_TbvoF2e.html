Alright, buckle up, because I'm about to deliver some stone-cold logo critiques. No holds barred. 1. Steaua Bucuresti: • Rating: 5/10 • Brutal Honesty: This logo screams "generic Eastern European football." The big "S" inside a circle is unimaginative and lacks any real punch. The three stars are cliché for signaling past championships. The color bars at the bottom look like an afterthought. The overall design is bland and forgettable. It's not offensive, but it's definitely not inspiring. 2. RB Leipzig: • Rating: 2/10 • Brutal Honesty: This logo is an abomination. It's corporate, soulless, and completely devoid of character. The two bulls facing each other are so generic they could be used for any energy drink. The "RB" is an obvious advertisement for Red Bull, and the font used for "LEIPZIG" is utterly boring. This logo represents everything that's wrong with modern football: commercialization, lack of history, and disregard for tradition. A total eyesore. 3. Girona FC: • Rating: 6/10 • Brutal Honesty: A solid, respectable logo. The design is clean and simple, with a clear connection to the city's coat of arms. The red and yellow color scheme is bold and visually appealing. The use of geometric shapes gives it a sense of stability and tradition. While it's not the most innovative logo, it's well-executed and effectively represents the club. 4. Red Bull Salzburg: • Rating: 3/10 • Brutal Honesty: Slightly better than Leipzig, but still pretty bad. The bulls are there again, and now there's a pointless ball. I get it that it's supposed to be 'Red Bull Soccer', but the design is still extremely corporate and uninspired. A complete lack of originality. It tells you nothing about the place or anything that would be soccer related. 5. Slovan Bratislava: • Rating: 8/10 • Brutal Honesty: This is a good logo. There's clearly a story behind this, with that cross. It is very authentic. The colors also make it look great, with the light blue and red. 6. BSC Young Boys: • Rating: 6/10 • Brutal Honesty: It's decent. At least they're known for their youth recruitment and this would be a perfect logo to associate with it. The yellow letters are also what makes it stand out, along with the black background.
