Alright, here's a tracklist for that absolutely bonkers collaboration, holding nothing back: Title: Meltdown 1. "Diamond Tears (Intro)" - Rihanna, El Profesor: A slow, haunting intro with Rihanna's vocals layered over El Profesor's melancholic guitar. It sets the stage for the chaotic energy to come. 2. "Firestorm" - Waterflame, KSI: Pure, unadulterated rage. Waterflame delivers a ridiculously aggressive dubstep beat while KSI unleashes a relentless barrage of diss tracks aimed at every single one of his rivals. This is the "hold no punches" anthem. 3. "Rasputin's Revenge" - Boney M., Black Kray: A twisted, dark reimagining of "Rasputin." Boney M. provides the eerie, choral vocals, while Black Kray delivers distorted, mumbled verses about paranoia and drug-fueled nightmares. 4. "Hard Knock Life" - Survivor, Talk Talk: This is the "underdog" track. Survivor lays down an anthem full of emotion. Talk Talk adds an introspective feel that only they can add to it. 5. "Umbrella Bomb" - Rihanna, Waterflame: This track is just pure power. Rhianna sings, while Waterflame drops bombs of EDM sounds. 6. "Money Heist Anthem" - El Profesor, Boney M.: El Profesor provides a suspenseful, Latin-infused melody. Boney M. chants in multiple languages, adding to the sense of global chaos. 7. "Red Alert" - KSI, Survivor: KSI brings high-energy, rapid-fire verses about facing down his enemies. Survivor comes in with a powerful chorus about resilience and never giving up, adding some grit and determination to the track. 8. "Tokyo Nights" - 天気予報, Black Kray: A dark, atmospheric trap banger. 天気予報 contributes ethereal vocals and traditional Japanese instruments, while Black Kray whispers fragmented verses about isolation and excess. 9. "Don't Stop Believin'" - Survivor, George Martin: A tribute song to each other, where George Martin provides his instrumental expertise and survivor sings the classic banger. 10. "Rain Tears" - 天気予報, Rihanna: A fusion of J-Pop, R&B, and traditional sounds, with Rihanna and 天気予報 trading verses about love, loss, and the changing seasons. 11. "Last Breath" - All Artists: A climactic, apocalyptic track where everyone throws down their best. It's dark, twisted, and utterly unforgettable. There you have it. An album that is sure to melt faces.
